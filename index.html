About GarageIntel

GarageIntel is an AI-powered assistant that transforms your workshop. Mechanics simply text a number-plate on WhatsApp, and receive a comprehensive vehicle profile, MOT history, and prioritized fault analysis in seconds.

Our service delivers up to 50% faster diagnostics and 30% fewer follow-up calls, while integrated booking, reminders, and invoicing streamline your daily operations.

Designed for garages aiming to stand out, GarageIntel enhances customer trust, boosts revenue, and future-proofs your business.
