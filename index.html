About GarageIntel

GarageIntel empowers garages to deliver exceptional customer satisfaction by providing instant, AI-driven vehicle insights via WhatsApp. From detailed MOT history to common fault analysis and guided troubleshooting, mechanics can diagnose and communicate with customers faster, reducing wait times and enhancing trust.

Designed for scalability, GarageIntel also includes booking, reminders, and invoicing features, making it the ultimate all-in-one assistant for garages looking to modernize operations and attract new business.
