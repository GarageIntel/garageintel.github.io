About GarageIntel

GarageIntel is a game-changing AI assistant that transforms your workshop workflow. Mechanic's simply text a number plate on WhatsApp, and instantly receive a full vehicle profile, MOT history, and prioritized fault analysis. Then, seamlessly schedule bookings, automate reminders, and issue invoices—all in one platform.

By reducing diagnostic time by up to 50% and cutting follow-up calls by 30%, GarageIntel not only accelerates repair turnaround but also elevates customer trust and loyalty. Equip your garage with the future of vehicle servicing. Boost efficiency, cut costs, and stand out in a competitive market.
